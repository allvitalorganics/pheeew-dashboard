The Problem

Gut health is the fastest-growing category in CPG wellness. The consumer intent is there, but the daily-use format does not exist. Most products ask people to take a pill, mix a powder, or drink a shot they do not want to drink again.

01

Powders get abandoned

Chalky texture, requires mixing, tastes like sand. Used once, pushed to the back of the cabinet within a month.

02

Wellness shots taste medicinal

Harsh ginger or herbal blends priced as occasional treats. Cold-chain only, so retail distribution is capped at refrigerated grocery.

03

Functional sodas are half-functional

Poppi and Olipop deliver prebiotic fiber in a craveable format. Neither delivers a probiotic. The full synbiotic stack exists only in capsule form.

Three-Year Channel Roadmap

Scale through the same specialty-to-mass retail path that carried Poppi from launch to a $1.95B PepsiCo acquisition in four years (CNBC, March 2025) and Olipop to a $1.85B valuation at Series C (Food Dive, 2025).

Year 1

DTC foundation plus Amazon

pheeew.com on Shopify. Subscription. LA farmer's markets as brand theater. Amazon listing with Subscribe & Save from Month 6. Target: first subscriber cohort, repeat purchase validation.
Precedent · AG1 ran DTC-only for a decade. Olipop ran DTC alongside specialty retail from 2018.
Year 2

Specialty grocery and niche retail

Erewhon (LA premium flagship). Whole Foods lighthouse placement. Sprouts regional natural chain. Target Takeoff emerging-brand program. Regional co-ops and independent natural stores.
Precedent · Poppi reached Target through Target Takeoff in 2021. Olipop reached Whole Foods in 2019–2020.
Year 3

Mass retail and club

Target national. CVS and Walgreens. Costco club. Walmart conventional. Amazon 1P with velocity proof. Channel diversification reduces concentration risk.
Precedent · Poppi entered Walmart 2023, acquired by PepsiCo March 2025 at $1.95B (3.9x revenue).
Economics built for scale. Unit economics at 40K MOQ already support retail wholesale margins (35–45% after retailer markup). At 100K+ volume commit, factory quote shows COGS drop of 15–20% per pouch, fully funding specialty retail slotting and mass retail trade spend without compressing contribution margin below 30% at retail.
