01

The Problem

Gut health is the fastest-growing category in CPG wellness. Every year consumers buy more probiotic capsules, collagen powders, and prebiotic sodas. Every year the same pattern repeats: people buy with intent, use for a week, and stop.

Powders taste chalky and require mixing. Used once, abandoned on the counter. Compliance is the single biggest failure point in wellness CPG.
Shots and bottled drinks are occasional. Priced like a treat ($4–$6), consumed like one. The format does not support daily use.
Prebiotic sodas are only half-functional. Poppi and Olipop deliver fiber but no probiotic. The full synbiotic stack exists only in capsule form.

Go-to-Market and Channel Roadmap

Launch through DTC and LA farmer's markets. Scale through the same specialty-to-mass retail path that carried Poppi from launch to a $1.95B PepsiCo acquisition in four years (CNBC, March 2025) and Olipop to a $1.85B valuation at Series C (Food Dive, 2025).

Part A · Launch Sprint (6 Months)

Month 1
Deploy capitalWire factory deposit for Wild Berries 40K MOQ. Order pouches (120K procurement). Alex and Dawon arrive in Los Angeles. Sample batch air-shipped from Korea.
Months 2–4
Validate in parallelTwo LA farmer's markets per week with sample pouches. Pre-order page live at pheeew.com. 30-creator seed wave. Community warm-up on IG and TikTok. Email list build.
Month 4–5
Launch DTCMain 40K shipment lands in LA. 3PL intake. Full Shopify launch. Subscription stack live (Recharge). Scaled Meta and TikTok paid. First subscription cohort.
Month 6
Decide nextSignal review. Commit to Phase 2 SKU (Tropical Punch) if repeat purchase and subscription signals hold. Begin prep for Angel extension round (H1 2027).

Part B · Multi-Year Channel Roadmap

Year 1
DTC foundation + Amazon
pheeew.com on Shopify. Subscription (Recharge). LA farmer's markets as brand theater. Amazon listing from Month 6 with Subscribe & Save. Target: first subscriber cohort, repeat purchase validation, early retail buyer conversations.
Precedent: AG1 ran DTC-only for a decade before retail. OLIPOP validated DTC + specialty in parallel from 2018.
Year 2
Specialty grocery and niche retail
Erewhon (LA premium flagship). Whole Foods lighthouse placement. Sprouts regional natural chain. Target Takeoff emerging-brand program. Regional co-ops and independent naturals.
Precedent: Poppi reached Target through Target Takeoff in 2021, 18 months after Series A. OLIPOP reached Whole Foods in 2019-2020.
Year 3
Mass retail and club
Target national. CVS and Walgreens drug channel. Costco club. Walmart conventional. Amazon 1P conversion with velocity proof. Channel diversification reduces single-channel concentration risk.
Precedent: Poppi entered Walmart 2023, PepsiCo acquired March 2025 at $1.95B (3.9x revenue, CNBC). Olipop at $1.85B valuation on similar path (Food Dive).
Economics built for scale. Unit economics at the 40K MOQ already support retail wholesale margins (35-45% after retailer markup). At 100K+ volume commit, factory quote shows COGS drop of 15-20% per pouch, which fully funds specialty retail slotting and mass retail trade spend without compressing contribution margin below 30% at retail. Sources: 좋은영농 factory quote (April 2026, volume pricing), Auxo Capital Food & Beverage Valuation Multiples 2026, BOTH_STRATEGY Brand Valuation CPG.
