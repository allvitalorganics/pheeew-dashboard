Product

100mL puree pouch · synbiotic · shelf-stable.

Format

Stand-up spout pouch. Shelf-stable (no refrigeration). One pouch per day. Drink in under thirty seconds. Mobile, one-handed.

Function

Synbiotic — prebiotic fiber + live probiotic — the clinically supported gut-health pairing. Not prebiotic-only (Poppi, Olipop). Not postbiotic (a different category entirely).

Three SKUs

  • Wild Berries — Gut + Energy · Phase 1 (launch)
  • Tropical Punch — Gut + Digestion · Phase 2
  • Ginger Pear — Gut + Immunity · Phase 3
